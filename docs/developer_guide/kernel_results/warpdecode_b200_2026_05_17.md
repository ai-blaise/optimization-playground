# WarpDecode B200 Result, 2026-05-17

## Scope

WarpDecode MoE kernel work for DeepSeek-style MoE decode on Blackwell. The
production dimensions are hidden size 7168, intermediate size 2048, top-k 8,
and 128 experts.

## Environment

- Hardware: NVIDIA B200 (`sm100`)
- Kernel type: CuTe/native CUDA
- Profiling: CUDA-event harnesses and Nsight kernel attribution
- Privacy: synthetic tensors only

## Current Result

Two baselines matter:

| Comparison | Baseline | Final | Speedup |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Original Triton fallback tile chain, B1 harness | 1567.8 us | 322.6 us | 4.86x |
| Production B1 Triton fallback | 307.9 us | 120.2 us CuTe path | 2.56x |

Final accepted gate/up 4-warp retune against direct CuTe control:

| Batch | Direct CuTe Control | Final Gate/Up 4-Warp | Speedup |
| ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 1 | 114.8 us | 113.6 us | 1.010x |
| 4 | 395.5 us | 393.9 us | 1.004x |
| 8 | 781.6 us | 773.5 us | 1.010x |
| 16 | 1543.7 us | 1528.9 us | 1.010x |
| 32 | 3082.7 us | 3033.9 us | 1.016x |
| 64 | 6156.1 us | 6095.8 us | 1.010x |

Final Nsight at B1 measured gate/up `<4,1024,4>` at 69.3 us average and down
`<8,2048,8>` at 38.5 us average. The pre-change CuTe path was 71.0 us and
38.6 us for the same kernels.

## Accepted Changes

- Promote the CuTe WarpDecode path over the Triton fallback for the supported
  production shape.
- Keep the gate/up 4-warp retune because it beat direct CuTe control across the
  measured batch range.

## Rejected Candidates

| Candidate | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Further launch and tile-shape probes after the 4-warp retune | Rejected where they tied or regressed against the accepted CuTe incumbent. |
| Scalar CUDA W4A4 inner-loop path | Not promoted because correctness was ready but performance stayed about 10x slower due to FP32 inner-loop work. |

## Verification

- WarpDecode focused tests passed in the final integration bundle.
- Quantization dispatch was covered in the GPU1 integration group.
- Final GPU1 bundle: 64 passed.

## Limits And Next Work

Additional material wins need tensor-op work in the remaining slow paths,
especially any W4A4/NVFP4 path still relying on scalar FP32 inner loops.
